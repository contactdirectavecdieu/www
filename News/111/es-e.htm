A New Book Series from Master to the World
The Supreme Master Ching Hai 1993 World Lecture Tour
Now Available in Both English and Chinese Editions

For the first time since publication of "The Key of Immediate Enlightenment" series, a new book collection is now available that brings us the inspiration of Master's life and teachings. Tracing Her travels through an astounding 24 cities over a mere three-month time period, this six-volume series contains all 28 lectures and selected interviews given by Supreme Master Ching Hai during Her unforgettable 1993 World Lecture Tour.

Unlike other books that convey noble ideas or philosophies, this collection provides more than a set of mental concepts. As a living Master, The Supreme Master Ching Hai has dedicated Her life to helping us uncover the nobility of our own true Self. This six-book series offers yet another glimpse into the message that She so lovingly shares with people from all walks of life and around the world. Her words illuminate the soul, revealing jewels of immeasurable value. And you can just turn a page to access these treasures again and again any time you need a reminder of God's love, compassion and wisdom.


The newly published Chinese version of "The Supreme Master Ching Hai 1993 World Lecture Tour" is now available!
